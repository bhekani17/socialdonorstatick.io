Social Donors Service

Social Donors is a not-for-profit organisation; therefore, ownership of its assets does not vest in its members. In the event that Socail Donor should stop operating, its assets would have to be transferred to an organisation with similar goals and objectives.

Socila Donors provides an essential service within South Africa and is rated amongst the best in the world in the provision of blood and blood products, as well as in relation to the research and training provided.

Social Donors operates across all of South Africa, with the exclusion of the Western Cape.

Social Donors is further regarded as a major role player in the provision of support to countries in the SADC region.

What is your type?

The differences in human blood are due to the presence or absence of certain protein molecules called antigens and antibodies. The antigens are located on the surface of the red blood cells and the antibodies are in the blood plasma.

Individuals have different types and combinations of these molecules. blood group you belong to depends on what you have inherited from your parents. To date, more than 20 genetically determined blood group systems exist, but the AB0 and Rh blood group systems are the most important ones used for blood transfusions.

blood groups are compatible with each other. Mixing incompatible blood groups leads to blood clumping, or agglutination, which is dangerous for individuals.
